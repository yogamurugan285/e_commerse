No cost EMI

No-cost EMI is a financing option that lets you buy something and pay for it in equal monthly installments without any interest or processing fees. It's different from regular EMIs, where you pay interest in addition to the price of the item.The "trick" behind no-cost EMIs is that merchants or lenders absorb the interest cost, allowing them to advertise "zero interest" while still making a profit, often through processing fees or by including the interest in the product's price

EMI on debit cards

Debit Card EMI allows you to convert high-value purchases into monthly installments, similar to credit card EMIs, without needing a credit card. You can choose a repayment tenure (3-24 months) and the EMI amount is automatically deducted from your savings account.

Mobile Exchange Offers

Yes, you can likely exchange a phone you purchased on EMI, but it depends on the terms and conditions of the exchange program and the specific retailer. You'll need to pay off any outstanding EMI installments before exchanging the phone.
